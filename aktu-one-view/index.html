Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU)

Established in 2000, AKTU aims to provide quality technical education in Uttar Pradesh, India.

Overview

AKTU, located in Lucknow, offers various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across multiple disciplines.Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University is committed to providing high-quality technical education, fostering innovation, and preparing students for successful careers. Its comprehensive approach to education and emphasis on research and industry collaboration make it a prominent institution in India.

Courses Offered

Undergraduate Programs

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
  • Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)
  • Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM)
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Bachelor of Business Management (BBM)

Postgraduate Programs

  • Master of Technology (M.Tech)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm)
  • Master of Hotel Management (MHM)

Doctoral Programs
